--- layout: speaker name: Yannick Verbelen bio: With a broad interest in sciences and technology, Yannick Verbelen has been fascinated by physics, chemistry and programming from early high school. He studied Electronic Engineering at the Erasmus University College of Brussels and proceeded to become a full time electronics designer and teacher at the Vrije Universiteit Brussel while, in his rare moments of free time, working on a PhD. In Fablab Brussels, Yannick is responsible for the stimulation and expansion of circuit board prototyping and encouraging interest in electronics. His interests primarily focus on mixed signal embedded systems with a preference for mechatronics and FPGA design. hosts: [ "Fireside Gathering", "Basic Oscilloscope Skills" ] ---
