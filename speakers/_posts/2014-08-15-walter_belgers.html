--- layout: speaker name: Walter Belgers bio: Walter Belgers is partner at Madison Gurkha, a Dutch security company, where he is managing teams of penetration testers. He likes to give trainings and lectures, including at conferences. Apart from his work, Walter has hobbies such as drifting, sailing and lock picking. He has been actively lock picking since last century and is currently president of the Dutch lock picking group Toool (The Open Organisation of Lock pickers). He has won 7 of the last 8 Dutch club competitions as fastest lock picker. hosts: [ "Lock Picking 101", "Lock Picking Workshop" ] ---
