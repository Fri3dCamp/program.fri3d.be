--- layout: event title: Vibro-Robots abstract: Create the best Vibro-Robot of all times!
A Vibro-Robot is a robot that is moved by a vibrating (eccentric) motor. In this workshop participants will learn how to build a circuit with a motor and a battery. Be ready to be amazed by the artistic skills of your kid. With wobbly eyes and pens are legs they float over the floor on little brushes. Anything goes! You decide what your little robot looks like, picking from the material that is available. host: Maria-Cristina Ciocci location: Children's Corner start: TBD duration: 90 min audience: kids ---
